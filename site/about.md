Gilberto Taccari is an IT executive and senior engineering leader with 10+ years
of experience leading innovation and scaling technology in European fintech.
His broad background underpins the core expertise in the areas below:

- **Strategic Leadership:** Tech Strategy & Roadmap, Team Scaling, Technical Due
  Diligence.
- **Fintech Domain:** Open Banking, Digital Banking, KYC/KYB Processes, Credit
  Automation.
- **Architecture & Culture:** AI-Native Engineering, Distributed Systems,
  Architecture Governance (ADRs), Domain-Driven Design (DDD).
- **Compliance & Security:** GDPR, PSD2, Financial Sector Regulations.

As **CTO** at **Tot**, Gilberto leads technology strategy and manages
engineering to support growth. He has supported an
**AI-native engineering culture** through integrating LLM-driven workflows to
boost efficiency while maintaining compliance with sector regulations and
security standards.

Prior to Tot, as **Co-Founder and Head of Technology** at **faire.ai**, Gilberto
developed an **AI-powered credit scoring platform**, secured early-stage funding
by communicating technical vision and leading technical due diligence, and
established strategic B2B partnerships in the **Open Banking** ecosystem.

Earlier leadership experience includes serving as **Tech Lead** at **Penta**
(acquired by Qonto) and as **Lead Backend Engineer** at **Infinitec Solutions**,
where he scaled distributed teams, promoted the use of Architecture Decision
Records (ADRs), and implemented Domain-Driven Design (DDD) to build scalable,
maintainable systems.

Alongside these core roles, Gilberto is a **Technical Reviewer** for
**Manning Publications Co.**, reviewing manuscripts on API Security,
Architecture Modernisation, and Engineering Management to secure alignment with
global best practices.

Gilberto holds a **PhD in Computer Engineering** and has expertise related to
distributed systems and cloud architectures. He drives business results,
engineering excellence, and strategic-oriented innovation by empowering
high-efficiency teams.
