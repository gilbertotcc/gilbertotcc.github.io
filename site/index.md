## About

> Currently: CTO at Tot., scaling engineering during a high-growth phase
> following a €7M pre-series A round.

I began my career as a software engineer, building trading platforms and banking
systems, which provided a robust foundation for regulated, high-stakes
applications. I then moved into technical leadership within the fintech startup
ecosystem, first as a tech lead at Penta (acquired by Qonto) and later as a
co-founder and Head of Technology at faire.ai, where I built an AI-powered
credit-scoring platform.

At Tot., my work as CTO focuses on three priorities: expanding engineering teams
to address real-world challenges, developing scalable systems to avoid technical
debt, and integrating AI into development workflows to enable rapid delivery. My
approach is informed by analytical discipline gained through a PhD in Computer
Engineering and academic research.
